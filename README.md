TASK 01- Business Sales Dashboard from E-commerce Data

The **goal** of the project is to find the following:

1. What are the best-selling products?
2. When do sales peak during the year?
3. Which categories or regions bring the most revenue?

**Key insights:**
**Category Insights**
• Technology generates the highest sales and profit.
• Office Supplies delivers strong profit with moderate sales.
• Furniture shows high sales but very low profit, indicating margin issues.

**Regional Insights**
• The West region leads in total sales.
• The South region shows the lowest performance and may need targeted strategies.

**Monthly Trends**
• Sales peak in November and December, driven by year-end/festive demand.
• February and January record the lowest sales levels.

**Business Recommendations:**
• Improve Furniture profitability by reviewing discounts and sourcing.
• Strengthen sales efforts in the South region.
• Maximise inventory and marketing before November–December.
• Promote Technology category bundles to leverage strong demand.
